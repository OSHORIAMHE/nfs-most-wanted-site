About Need for Speed: Most Wanted (2012)

Need for Speed: Most Wanted (2012) delivers an open-world street racing experience set in the sprawling city of Fairhaven. Unlike traditional racing games, you have the freedom to explore the city, discover hidden cars, and challenge the elite Most Wanted racers at your own pace.

The game features a dynamic police pursuit system where cops use smart tactics and roadblocks to stop you, creating intense high-speed chases. With the innovative EasyDrive system, you can seamlessly swap cars and upgrade performance mods on the fly without interrupting gameplay.
